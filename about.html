Who Are We?

        We are Stanford's first official student group focusing on the intersection of computer science and social impact. OUR MISSION To empower students to leverage technology for social good by inspiring action, facilitating collaboration, and forging pathways towards change. WHAT WE DO We encourage students at all levels of engagement to find ways to combine their passions for CS and social good. We do so by: Motivating CS students to apply their skills for social change Building a community of students excited about improving lives with technology Create opportunities for interested students to engage in the space Providing resources for students to more effectively hack for social good.
